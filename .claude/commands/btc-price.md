# Get BTC & Crypto Market Snapshot

When I run this command, do the following:

## 1. Current BTC price
- Fetch the **latest Bitcoin (BTC) price** in USD (and optionally another fiat if relevant).
- Show it clearly with a brief “as of” or “live” note.

## 2. BTC movement (last 24 hours)
- Get Bitcoin’s **price change in the last 24 hours** (absolute and percentage).
- Indicate direction (up/down) and whether it’s above or below the 24h high/low if available.

## 3. Overall crypto market movement (from now, look back)
Report how the **broader crypto market** has moved over these windows (use a major index like total market cap or a basket if possible; otherwise summarize from BTC + a few majors):

| Window       | What to show                          |
|-------------|----------------------------------------|
| Last 24 hrs | Change % and brief note                |
| Last week   | Change % and brief note                |
| Last month  | Change % and brief note                |
| Last 3 months | Change % and brief note             |
| Last 6 months | Change % and brief note             |
| Last year   | Change % and brief note                |

Present this in a compact table or bullet list so it’s easy to scan.

## 4. Recent movement & news
- Summarize **recent price action or volatility** (e.g. sharp moves, support/resistance levels) if notable.
- List **2–5 recent news or events** (regulatory, macro, exchange, adoption, hacks, etc.) that are likely affecting the market.
- Keep each item to 1–2 sentences; add dates/sources when helpful.

## How to get the data
- Use **web search** and/or **fetching reliable URLs** (e.g. CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap, Bloomberg Crypto, major exchanges, or crypto news sites) to obtain:
  - Live/current BTC price and 24h stats.
  - Historical snapshots or charts for 1w, 1m, 3m, 6m, 1y.
  - Recent headlines and market commentary.
- Prefer authoritative or widely cited sources; if you can’t get exact numbers, say so and give a range or “approximately” with the source.

## Output format
- Start with **BTC now** and **BTC 24h**.
- Then the **market movement table** (24h → 1y).
- End with **Recent movement & news** in a short, scannable list.
- Keep the whole response concise; use bullets and tables rather than long paragraphs.
